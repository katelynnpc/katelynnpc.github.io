Music & Theatre

My theatre opportunities have been amazing!


Theatre started for me in high school where I got my first ever role as a supporting lead which helped start my love for theatre. Since then I have participated in many other high school productions as well as a bunch at a local community theater called Red Curtain Foundation for the Arts located in Marysville Washington.

I have been playing saxophone since 5th grade and still play.


I have been a part of many ensembles througout middle school and in high school I was in my band's wind ensemble as well as jazz band. My sophopmore year I joined the jazz choir and continued to be in three music groups for the rest of my high school years. My senior year I was chosen to be the Northwest Honor Band representative where I went to Pacific Lutheran University where I got to play with some of the best high school musicians in the pacific northwest states. At the end of my senior year I won the instrumentalist of the year award


In college at Western Washington University I have joined the WWU Symphonic Band where I join over 100 other students to perform a concert at the end of every quarter.
